Privacy Policy

Who I Am

My name is Paul Rushforth, and I offer coaching, facilitation, and consultancy services through Breathe & Smile. I am based in the UK and comply with UK GDPR and Data Protection regulations.

What Information I Collect

  • Name, email address, phone number
  • Job title, organisation, or relevant background
  • Any other information you provide via forms, emails, or in sessions

How I Use Your Information

  • To respond to enquiries
  • To deliver coaching, facilitation, or consultancy services
  • To send agreed-upon updates or information
  • To improve my services and understand client needs

I will never sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Legal Basis for Processing

I collect and process personal data based on:

  • Consent (e.g. when you fill in a form)
  • Contract (e.g. when you become a client)
  • Legitimate interest (e.g. to improve services or respond to enquiries)

How Your Data is Stored

Your information is stored securely and only for as long as needed. This includes encrypted cloud storage and secure client records. I review data retention regularly.

Your Rights

  • Access your data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the ICO if needed

Cookies & Website Analytics

My website may use basic cookies or analytics tools (e.g. Google Analytics) to understand usage patterns. You can disable cookies in your browser.

Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will always be available on my website or upon request.
