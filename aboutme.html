My Story

Some sort of subtitle wil go in here

Change has been the constant thread throughout my career. I’ve spent over 24 years navigating, facilitating, and driving transformation across industries, teams, and technologies. Whether it’s helping organisations adapt to disruptive innovations or empowering individuals to find clarity in moments of transition, I’ve built a career around problem-solving, adaptability, and understanding people.
Throughout my career, I’ve helped leaders and teams navigate waves of technological transformation. From driving the adoption of video podcasting at the Syfy Channel during the rise of social media, to guiding the transition from physical media to a fully digital, tapeless post-production workflow, I’ve been at the forefront of change. I’ve worked with businesses to embrace innovations like streaming services, virtual reality, and artificial intelligence, ensuring they stay ahead of the curve and resilient in shifting markets. I’m also proud to call myself an “inventor,” with a patent pending for an autonomous camera control system I developed with colleagues at NEP.
Problem-solving is at the heart of everything I do. I take complex, messy ideas, systems, technologies, or situations and find ways to simplify and clarify. By breaking challenges down into manageable parts, I uncover the best approaches to move forward effectively and with purpose. Throughout these experiences, I’ve gained a deep understanding of how people navigate change and uncertainty—the fears, motivations, and strengths that come into play during times of transition. This insight enables me to build trust quickly, foster collaboration, and guide individuals and teams toward meaningful outcomes.
Today, as a coach and mentor, I help clients embrace change and unlock their potential. I’m passionate about empowering others to see opportunities in uncertainty, navigate transitions with confidence, and align their goals with their values. Whether it’s navigating external changes or creating internal transformation, I’m here to guide you through the process and help you achieve lasting growth.
