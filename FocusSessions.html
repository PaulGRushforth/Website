Focus Sessions

Feeling Stuck in Your Own Head?

Sometimes, the hardest part of dealing with a challenge, opportunity, or decision is finding the headspace to think it through. Life moves fast, distractions are everywhere, and even when we do make time, it’s easy to get stuck in loops—overthinking, second-guessing, or going round in circles on our own.

That’s where a Focus Session can help.

What is a Focus Session?

A Focus Session is a one-off, 60- or 90-minute coaching conversation designed to give you structured, supportive space to think clearly. Whether you're navigating an unexpected situation, wrestling with a tough decision, or need a moment to untangle your thoughts, this session helps you move forward with clarity and confidence.

No long-term commitment. Just practical support, when you need it most.

How It Works

In a Focus Session, we’ll:

  • Explore your challenge — what’s happening, what matters, and what might be holding you back
  • Shift perspective — unpack assumptions, spot patterns, and surface new ways of seeing the situation
  • Create a plan — develop clear, doable next steps so you leave with focus and direction

You’ll walk away with a clearer mind, renewed energy, and a practical way forward—all from a single, focused session.

Who It’s For

A Focus Session is for you if:

  • You’re stuck in a situation and want to break through the mental fog
  • You’re facing a decision and need to talk it through
  • You need a sounding board for ideas, options, or possibilities
  • You don’t want ongoing coaching but would value a single session of focused support

If you're not sure how coaching can help, check out the One to One Coaching Page for more details.

Focus sessions are available to book in single units when you need them, or in discounted packages.
